This article explores seven alternatives to Appsmith for building internal tools, noting that the rise of generative AI has transformed the internal tool development landscape since Appsmith's peak popularity. The seven alternatives examined are Budibase, ToolJet, OutSystems, Mendix, Retool, Power Apps, and JetAdmin, each assessed for distinct advantages ranging from no-code simplicity to enterprise-grade AI capabilities. The guide helps teams evaluate which platform fits their organizational needs and technical requirements.
